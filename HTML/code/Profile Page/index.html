Bio:Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is a renowned Indian cricketer and a right-handed batsman. He gained prominence by leading India to victory in the 2008 U-19 World Cup. Kohli made his ODI debut in 2008, Test debut in 2011, and T20I debut in 2010. Known for his aggressive batting style and consistency, he holds numerous records, including being the fastest to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 runs in ODIs. Kohli has been associated with Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL since 2008 and is their leading run-scorer.
