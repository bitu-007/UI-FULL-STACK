Virat Kohli
The Greatest Of All Time

The Greatest in Cricket History

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is a celebrated cricketer known for his aggressive batting style and exceptional leadership. He captained the Indian national team across all formats and is regarded as one of the best batsmen of his generation. Kohli has numerous records, including the fastest to 8,000, 9,000, and 10,000 runs in ODIs. His passion, fitness, and consistency have earned him numerous accolades, including the ICC Cricketer of the Year. Beyond cricket, Kohli is involved in philanthropy and endorses various brands, making him a prominent figure both on and off the field.
